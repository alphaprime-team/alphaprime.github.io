Elements To Our Success

Building foundations of the next generation in rescue service robots require mastery of the following elements.

Estimate

Using Extended Kalman Filter Packages we can employ cutting edge sensor fusion to determine accurate attitude and position.

Control

Using multiple control packages, our drones are capable of stabilizing through any trajectory. We achieve steady state at incredible accuracy.

Map

No matter what the situation. As soon as Alpha Prime drones lose GPS access, a mental map leads them back home.

Plan

A combination of advanced machine learning techniques and an advanced nagivation stack generates waypoints in realtime.

Climatize

Climatic Wind Tunnel

The team has access to multiple laboratories and research labs such as a climatic wind tunnel and a UAV proving ground to test Alpha Prime Rescue drones against vast environmental conditions.

The unique opportunity presented by access to this climatic wind tunnel lets us test our hardware including battery performance in unprecedented conditions.

Realize

Agile The Best Ideas

Traditionally, rework of physical projects would be the one of the largest costs, so classical project management would require us to stick to initial scope despite undiscovered optimizations.

Agile methodologies, we will focus on building multiple modular drone bodies to rapidly iterate and optimize for the physical flight of our robot.

Virtualize

Simulate Everything

Before Alpha Prime Drones touch our advanced hardware platform, the software is put through rigorous simluations and virtual environment. Our code is modular and is tested for safety at its very essence prior to inhabiting a physical body. Modules are tested indvidiually and then as a whole in flight goggles.                            

Climate Ready Before Taking Flight

In order to fully emulate real-world variations our team will be accessing the ACE (Automotive Centre of Excellence) Climatic Wind Tunnel. ACE is one of a kind, state of the art R&D facility that is the first of its kind in Canada, and in some aspects the world.The ACE Climatic Wind tunnel has the capability of simulating extreme weather conditions (from -40°C up to 60°C), replicating the effects of the sun with solar arrays and testing properties in crosswinds. This can prove to be very valuable when it comes to testing the drone in several complex conditions that it may have to overcome on a day to day basis. In addition to being able to test the kit in various different environments, access to the ACE Indoor UAV Proving Ground will allow us to setup various drone scenarios and circuits to harden our drones against the elements.
