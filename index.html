Remote Disaster Relief

Alpha Prime Rescue drones will target post-disaster areas with limited access to medicine and basic human necessities. The goal with Alpha Prime Rescue is to develop a drone that can fight forest fires as well as deliver much needed medication and basic human necessities to post-disaster and underserved areas of the world.
